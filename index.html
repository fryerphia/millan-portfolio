BookNest

BookNest is an all-in-one inventory management system that offers to streamline and automate the daily activities of a bookstore. The system has a centralized dashboard that enables the shop owners and staff to monitor their whole collection conveniently, leaving behind manual tracking and establishing a dynamic and digital space. The prototype will enable real-time inventory changes by establishing a powerful CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) system, so that the stock levels, price, and genres of the music are always correct. A very convenient system is able to reduce operational bottlenecks, and users are able to check the availability of books within seconds and have a very well-organized storefront.
